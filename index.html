About Us

La Luna at its heart is an expression of our passion for all things nature. We are a 10 acre farm with a mix of bushland and pasture with 2 acres of market garden at its centre growing a diverse range of veggies, fruits and flowers. Using organic principles we aim to feed the community a seasonal variety of nutrient dense produce whilst building back the land and its ecosystems. We are a small team who love what we do and appreciate being able to do it in such a beautiful part of the world.

Agriculture

Community Supported Agriculture (CSA) is a collaborative farming model that fosters a direct connection between consumers and local farms. In a CSA program, individuals or families purchase "shares" of a farm's harvest, acknowledging the sometimes unpredictable nature of small-scale organic growing. Throughout the season, members receive a portion of the farm's produce weekly or fortnightly. This mutually beneficial arrangement not only provides members with fresh, seasonal, and organic produce, but also supports local farmers by providing them with financial stability and a guaranteed market for their crops. Additionally, CSAs promote sustainable agriculture practices, strengthen community ties, and encourage a deeper understanding of where our food comes from.
